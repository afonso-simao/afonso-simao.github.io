Work

Portuguese Air Force (JUNE 2016 / JUNE 2023)

Communications Operator / Air Operations Assistant

During my lifetime in the Portuguese Air Force I worked in 2 military bases. From June 2016 to December 2019 I worked in Air Force Military and Technical Training Center, in Ota and from December 2019 to June 2023 I worked in Air Base nº5, in Monte Real.


As a Communications Operator / Air Operations Assistant:


  • Operate communication and information systems.
  • Execution of tasks related to the activity of air operations.
  • Technical and material resources in military operations.
  • Ensuring the success of the mission.
  • Definition and optimization of process.


Before I leave the institution, I received a public praise due to my dedication, tenacity, competence and zeal at work, showing excellent aptitude for the same. I showed a spirit of initiative, presented proposals for improvement and my dynamism contributed to the smooth functioning of the work. I also had relevant military and professional qualities, dedication to service, loyalty and a spirit of mutual help. (Some words written on my public praise).
